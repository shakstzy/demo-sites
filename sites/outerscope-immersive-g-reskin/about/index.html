About

Outer Scope builds the operating layer between people, tools, and AI.

Method

Map the work. Remove the handoffs. Ship the system.

We focus on the parts of the business that repeat, drift, or disappear into inboxes. The output is a working automation system: intake, CRM movement, audit reporting, follow-up, internal copilots, and operator dashboards.

Fit

Built for teams that already have momentum.

Outer Scope is strongest when the product works, the demand is real, and the back office needs to stop slowing down the front office.
