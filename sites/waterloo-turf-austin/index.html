How to Maintain Perfect Turf

Unlike natural grass that drains your time and bank account, synthetic turf requires exceptionally minimal maintenance to look fresh and professional all year.

Over time, heavy foot traffic or patio furniture might flatten turf fibers. A quick brush with a synthetic bristle broom (never use metal wires) easily lifts the blades back to their beautiful, upright position.

★ Pro tip: Brush against the grain of the blades for immediate vertical definition.

During dry Austin summers, dust and pollen can settle on the turf. A swift spray with a standard garden hose removes debris and restores the vibrant, pristine deep green shade of your lawn.

★ Pro tip: For pet owners, a quick rinse over heavy-use areas keeps everything perfectly odorless and fresh.

Unlike standard turf which fades, requires mowing, weed-eating, fertilization, and massive utility bills, Waterloo Turf requires no weeding, no cutting, and zero harmful chemicals. Enjoy a lush, emerald lawn 365 days a year.
