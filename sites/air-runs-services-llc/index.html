Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our heating, cooling, and maintenance offerings.

How often should my AC system receive professional maintenance?
For maximum efficiency and life extension, your central cooling system should be professionally serviced once a year, ideally in the spring. Heating systems should be tuned up in the autumn. Consistent maintenance keeps your system under manufacturer warranty active and prevents most unexpected breakdown calls.
Do you provide 100% free estimates?
Absolutely. We provide free on-site consultations and detailed project cost estimates for new AC installations, heating retrofits, complete system ductwork layout design, and mini-split setups. For general mechanical repair visits, contact our team directly for direct transparent pricing structures.
Are mini splits better than traditional ducted AC?
Mini splits offer outstanding targeted zone heating and cooling control and are highly energy efficient, as there is zero energy loss in ductwork. They represent the perfect solution for custom house additions, garage workspaces, and historical residences where ducting installation isn't possible. Traditional central systems remain the standard choice for full comprehensive whole-house airflow.
What are the signs that my water heater is failing?
Key indicators of water heater degradation include rusty/discolored hot water, banging or rumbling sounds from within the tank (indicating sediment layer buildup), persistent low water temperatures, and moisture pools around the base. If you notice leaks, contact our dispatch instantly to avoid major water damage.
